Ku's Laundromat – Coin Laundry & Wash and Fold in Oakland Gardens, New York

Ku's Laundromat is a full service laundromat serving Oakland Gardens and the surrounding New York area. We provide self service coin laundry, professional wash and fold service, and convenient drop-off laundry options. Our modern facility features commercial grade washers and dryers for all load sizes, from small loads to large comforters and bedding. Enjoy complimentary WiFi while you wait. Free parking available for all customers. Whether you need a quick self service wash or prefer our attendant assisted laundry service, we make doing laundry easy and convenient.

Ku's Laundromat offers free WiFi, on site parking, a coin changer, an on site attendant, and RV friendly access, making it a well equipped option for residents in Oakland Gardens, New York.
